24.6.3.5 views​::​iota [range.iota.adaptor]

1
#
The name views​::​iota denotes a customization point object ([customization.point.object]).
For some subexpressions E and F, the expressions views​::​iota(E) and views​::​iota(E, F) are expression-equivalent to iota_­view{E} and iota_­view{E, F}, respectively.
