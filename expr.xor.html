7 Expressions [expr]

7.6 Compound expressions [expr.compound]

7.6.12 Bitwise exclusive OR operator [expr.xor]

exclusive-or-expression:
	and-expression
	exclusive-or-expression ^ and-expression
1
#
The operands shall be of integral or unscoped enumeration type.
The usual arithmetic conversions ([expr.arith.conv]) are performed.
Given the coefficients xi and yi of the base-2 representation ([basic.fundamental]) of the converted operands x and y, the coefficient ri of the base-2 representation of the result r is 1 if either (but not both) of xi and yi are 1, and 0 otherwise.
[ Note
:
The result is the bitwise exclusive OR function of the operands.
— end note
 ]
