When a function is designated in a call, which function declaration is being referenced and the validity of the call are determined by comparing the types of the arguments at the point of use with the types of the parameters in the declarations in the overload set.
This function selection process is called overload resolution and is defined in [over.match].
[Note 2: 
Overload sets are formed by id-expressions naming functions and function templates and by splice-expressions designating entities of the same kinds.
