#
numpunct<> specifies numeric punctuation.
The specializations required in Table 104 ([locale.category]), namely numpunct<​wchar_t> and numpunct<char>, provide classic "C" numeric formats, i.e., they contain information equivalent to that contained in the "C" locale or their wide character counterparts as if obtained by a call to widen.
