1

9.7.2.2.2 back_insert_iterator::operator= [back.insert.iter.op=]

1

Effects: Equivalent to container->push_back(value).

2

Returns: *this.

3

Effects: Equivalent to container->push_back(std::move(value)).

4

Returns: *this.
