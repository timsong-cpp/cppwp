17.5.4.2.3 Namespaces for future standardization [namespace.future]

1
#

Top level namespaces with a name starting with std and followed by a non-empty sequence of digits are reserved for future standardization. The behavior of a C++ program is undefined if it adds declarations or definitions to such a namespace.
