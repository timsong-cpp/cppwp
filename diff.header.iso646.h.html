C.8.3.4 Header <iso646.h> [diff.header.iso646.h]

1
#
The tokens and, and_eq, bitand, bitor, compl, not, not_eq, or, or_eq, xor, and xor_eq are keywords in C++ ([lex.key]), and are not introduced as macros by <iso646.h>.
