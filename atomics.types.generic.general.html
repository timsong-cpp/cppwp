  • (1.1)

    is_­copy_­constructible_­v<T>,
  • (1.3)
    is_­move_­constructible_­v<T>,
  • (1.4)
    is_­copy_­assignable_­v<T>, or
  • (1.5)
    is_­move_­assignable_­v<T>
is false.
