23 Strings library [strings]

23.1 General [strings.general]

1
#
This Clause describes components for manipulating sequences of any non-array trivial standard-layout ([basic.types.general]) type.
Such types are called char-like types, and objects of char-like types are called char-like objects or simply characters.
